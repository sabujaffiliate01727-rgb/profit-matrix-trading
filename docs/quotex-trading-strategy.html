Strategy 1: Support & Resistance Bounce

This is the most reliable strategy for beginners and pros alike. It uses pure price action — no indicators needed.

  1. Identify key levels — Draw horizontal lines where price has bounced at least twice
  2. Wait for touch + confirmation — Price must touch the level AND form a reversal candle (engulfing, pin bar, or doji)
  3. Enter with 60s expiry — Trade in the bounce direction immediately after the confirmation candle closes
  4. Risk management — Never risk more than 2-5% of your balance per trade

Strategy 2: Moving Average Crossover

Add two moving averages to your chart: EMA 9 (fast) and EMA 21 (slow).

  • When EMA 9 crosses ABOVE EMA 21 → BUY
  • When EMA 9 crosses BELOW EMA 21 → SELL
  • Best on: EUR/USD, GBP/USD during London/New York sessions
  • Expiry: 2-5 minutes for best results

Strategy 3: RSI Divergence

RSI divergence is one of the most powerful reversal signals in trading.

  • Price makes a new high, but RSI makes a lower high → SELL signal
  • Price makes a new low, but RSI makes a higher low → BUY signal
  • Works best on 5-minute charts with 2-5 minute expiry
