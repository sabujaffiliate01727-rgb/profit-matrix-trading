What is Binary Trading?

Binary trading is simple: you predict whether an asset's price will go UP or DOWN within a set time (usually 60 seconds to 5 minutes). If you're right, you earn up to 80-95% profit.

Step 2: Learn a Strategy

Don't trade blindly. The most effective beginner strategy is the Support & Resistance + Engulfing Candle method:

  1. Draw horizontal lines at price levels where price bounced before
  2. Wait for price to touch these levels again
  3. Look for a strong reversal candle (engulfing pattern)
  4. Enter in the reversal direction with 60-second expiry

Common Beginner Mistakes

  • ❌ Trading without a strategy — always follow rules
  • ❌ Risking too much per trade — never risk more than 2-5% of your balance
  • ❌ Revenge trading — if you lose, take a break
  • ❌ Not using a demo first — practice before going live
